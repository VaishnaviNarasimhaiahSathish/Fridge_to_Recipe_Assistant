Return ONLY valid JSON.
Do not include explanations outside the JSON.
Do not wrap the JSON in markdown.

- Prefer specific ingredient names over broad categories.
- Include packaged food only when the label is readable or the visible contents clearly identify the item.
- Do not identify food only from container shape, bottle shape, jar shape, color, or common fridge assumptions.
- Do not guess hidden, blocked, blurry, or partly visible items.
- Do not infer common fridge items unless they are clearly visible.
- Do not list duplicate alternatives for the same item. For example, do not return both "butter" and "margarine" unless both are separately visible and clearly identifiable.

- Do not suggest recipes.
- Do not put generic names such as "leafy green vegetable", "red berry", "dark berry", "unknown bottle", "unknown jar", or "unknown packaged item" in "ingredients"; place them only in "uncertain_items".
